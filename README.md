🍰 Sugario – Bakery Website

📖 Overview
Sugario is a bakery-themed website that showcases freshly baked cakes, cookies, sandwiches, and breads. It highlights premium ingredients, warm branding, and a seamless browsing experience. The site is designed to engage visitors with clear navigation, appealing visuals, and strong calls to action.  

🌐 Pages
- Homepage – Hero cake banner with “Order Now” and “Read More” buttons.  
- Store – Categories for Cake, Cookie, Sandwich, and Bread.  
- About – Brand story, values, and baking philosophy.  
- Feature Food – Product listings with images and prices.  
- Best Seller – Weekly highlight with rating and “Buy Now” option.  
- App Promo – Teaser for mobile expansion with App Store & Google Play badges.  
- Contact – Services (Delivery, Dine-in, Takeaway), contact info, and newsletter subscription.  

🎨 Features
- Clean, minimal design with strong visuals.  
- Clear calls to action for ordering and subscribing.  
- Weekly best-seller promotions to keep content fresh.  
- Newsletter integration for customer engagement.  
- Contact details and service options for customer trust.  

🚀 Tech Stack
- HTML, CSS, JavaScript
